About Texture Packs Nepal

Welcome! Texture Packs Nepal creates custom Minecraft resource packs at affordable prices in NPR. We focus on delivering high-quality textures that improve immersion while keeping performance in mind.

Our services include: custom block & item textures, GUI redesigns, and full themed packs tailored to servers or individual creators. We work closely with clients to deliver exactly the style they imagine — from realistic to low-poly and everything between. To view our services, please proceed to the Services tab on the top of our website.
NOTE: Please do keep in mind that the whole website and business is operated by one single person and thus may not be able to produce results very quickly. We are actively trying to hire new members and graphic designers to help. Thank you!

Our Mission

To provide affordable, polished resource packs for Nepali Minecraft players,
enabling creators to stand out with unique visual identities.
